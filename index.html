A Brief Overview of Our Mission

Primary Mission: Our primary mission, as required, is to successfully and safely land the CanSat, as well as take measurements for air temperature and pressure. Secondary Mission: Our secondary mission aims to give us qualitative evidence about the suitability of a location for sustained human life, by providing analysis regarding the atmosphere of a location and the geography of a location, such as the availability of water and vegetation. This will be almost entirely software based, retrieving the local storage from the CanSat, and then plugging the information into a custom-built program for picture analysis.
